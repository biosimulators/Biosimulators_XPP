About BioSimulators-XPP¶

License¶

This package is released under the MIT License. XPP is released under the GPL 2.0 License.

Development team¶

This package was developed by the Karr Lab at the Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai and the Center for Reproducible Biomedical Modeling. XPP was developed by Bard Ermentrout at the University of Pittsburgh.

Questions and comments¶

Please contact the BioSimulators Team with any questions or comments about the command-line program or Docker image. Please address questions and comments about XPP to Bard Ermentrout.
